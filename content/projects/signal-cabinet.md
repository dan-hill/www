Signal Cabinet is the shared publishing rig that keeps the public surface and the docs surface reading like parts of the same machine.

## CURRENT ROLE

- shared theme delivery
- project row grammar
- text-first rendering
- no-JS baseline

## WHY IT MATTERS

The hard part is not shipping a theme. The hard part is keeping the shared layer expressive enough that downstream sites do not feel like generic templates in disguise.
