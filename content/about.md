This public site is a terminal-facing surface for projects, dispatches, and links out to deeper documentation.

## OPERATING IDEA

The goal is not "minimalism" in the polite startup sense. The goal is a readable board:

- text-first
- link-heavy
- fast to scan
- useful without JavaScript
- comfortable in a terminal brain

## WHY THIS SHAPE

A lot of sites hide the interesting parts behind screenshots, cards, and marketing copy. This one tries to do the opposite.

The important things should be legible immediately:

1. what exists
2. what is live
3. what is in progress
4. where the notes live
5. what changed recently

## ROUTING

If you are just dropping in:

- start with the **Project Index**
- check **Dispatches** for recent traces
- jump to **Docs** for the heavier reference layer
